Who We Are

Association of Civil Engineers Thirumangalam

The Association of Civil Engineers – Thirumangalam (ACET) is a professional body formed to unite civil engineers and promote safety, ethical practices, and technical excellence in the construction industry.

Established through the collective efforts of local engineers and officially registered in 2025, the association serves as a platform for knowledge sharing, professional development, and mutual support among civil engineering professionals.

ACET is proudly affiliated with the Federation of All Civil Engineering Associations of Tamil Nadu & Puducherry, strengthening collaboration and representation at the state level.

Our Story

The Journey of ACET

Formation & Vision

Local civil engineers came together with a shared vision to create a professional platform that would unite the engineering community in Thirumangalam and promote excellence in the construction industry.

Official Registration - 2025

ACET was officially registered as a professional body, marking the formal establishment of the association and laying the groundwork for organized professional development.

State-Level Affiliation

ACET became affiliated with the Federation of All Civil Engineering Associations of Tamil Nadu & Puducherry, strengthening our voice at the state level and expanding collaboration opportunities.

Growing Impact

Today, ACET continues to grow with 48+ active members, conducting regular meetings, professional development events, and contributing to the development of Thirumangalam and surrounding regions.
